INTRODUCTION

I'm Alozie Kemjika Perseverance, a multi-talented engineer with expertise in web/app development (full stack), piping engineering (pipe fitter/welder), video editing, etc. Highly driven by passion and enthusiasm, I offer real-life solutions through accumulated experience in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for frontend development, as well as PHP, MySQL, Node.js, etc., for backend development. The use of frameworks and libraries such as Laravel, React, and React Native is also not neglected, as they facilitate fast-paced designs and integration. In piping engineering, I am experienced in isometric drawing interpretation and fabrication as a pipe fitter, the use of various cutting machines including oxy-fuel cutting, and accurate pipe-to-fittings alignment. In welding, I am proficient in one to four fillet welding positions and one to six groove positions, all in shielded metal arc welding (SMAW). Once again, a warm welcome to working with you.
